struct pngpack_channel* pngpack_channel_new ( char *  name,
double *  data,
size_t  data_length 
)

This creates a channel structure and calculates statistics for the data passed.

When this structure is passed to pngpack_add_channel pngpack becomes the owner and will free it as part of pngpack_free. Otherwise, you may free it yourself.

The data passed remains owned by the caller, and must remain allocated throughout this structure's lifetime.
