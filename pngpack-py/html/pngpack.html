class pngpack.PngpackBounds¶

An object representing the bounds of a packed PNG file. These bounds represent the top-left and bottom-right pixels of the image, and allow you to give context to the packed data.

For example, if the packed data represented temperatures over a grid, the bounds may represent the max/min lat/lon of the grid.
